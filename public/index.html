Designed for chain and category-level location discovery

Use specific-chain exports when you know the brand. Use entire-category exports when you want all locations of a type in a country, such as all pharmacies, all fast food restaurants or all hotels.

🛒 Retail & grocery

Supermarkets, convenience stores, fashion, electronics, furniture, specialty retail and general shops.

🍔 Food & restaurants

⛽ Mobility & fuel

Gas stations, EV charging, car rental, car sharing, bike rental and automotive retail or service locations.

🏨 Hotels & services

Hotels, gyms, banks, parcel lockers, pharmacies, clinics, opticians and other consumer-facing service locations.
